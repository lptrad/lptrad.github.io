About Us

Leonardo Peres works as a translator since 2015. He has professional translator training from Brasillis Idiomas and a Post-Graduate Diploma in Translation Studies from the Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul, in Brazil.

Services

We offer English-Brazilian Portuguese and Brazilian Portuguese-English translations in the following areas:

Translation of legal documents.
Videogame localization.
Technical translations.
Information Technology translations.
Business translations: reports, presentations, releases, correspondence.
Subtitling of movies, institutional videos, and videos in general.
